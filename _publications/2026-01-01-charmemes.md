CHARMemes is a large-scale benchmark of 23,025 examples for harmful meme detection in the Runet (Russian-language internet) ecosystem. The dataset was constructed using LLM-assisted annotation and covers a range of harmful content categories in multimodal (image + text) meme format. We also present a LangGraph multi-agent system automating the MBFC (Media Bias/Fact Check) methodology for automated fact-checking, and fine-tuned DeBERTa-v3-Large classifiers for propaganda-technique identification that outperform a direct LLM baseline by 7% FACTScore.
